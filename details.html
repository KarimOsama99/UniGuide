Campuses of Ain-Shams University and the available specializations:

  1. The Main Campus
    This campus is located in Topkapi and covers a total area of 57200 square meters. It has 47 classrooms, 99 laboratories, 3 conference rooms, 5 architecture workshops, computer workshops, a cinema, television and radio studio, cafeterias, restaurants, libraries, study spaces, and many other facilities to meet the students' needs during their studies.
  2. Vadi Campus
    It is an impressive architectural building located south of the main campus.
  3. Third Campus
    It is under construction, and the university administration plans to make it an architectural masterpiece.
